Items on the map
   
   

The following items may be on the map:

•map (link to another map),

•partition,

•zone,

•camera.

Map

The link to another map is presented as a color polygon. Hovering the cursor over the polygon will display, in the bottom left corner of the window, the name of the map to which the link points. Click on the polygon to go to the map.

If the polygon overlaps with another item, clicking will display a pop-up menu containing, among other items, name of the map and command to display that map.

Partition

The partition is presented as a semi-transparent color polygon. How the partition status is indicated, see the table below (the polygon shape is just an example).

Polygon

Color

Partition status

gray

disarmed

blue

exit delay countdown is running

green

armed

red

alarm or alarm memory

orange

fire alarm or fire alarm memory

Hovering the cursor over the polygon will display the partition name in the bottom left corner of the window. Click on the polygon to display a pop-up menu. The pop-up menu contains:

•partition name,

•icon indicating the partition status (the same icons are used as on the control panel data screen in the “Structure” tab),

•command to arm / disarm the partition or clear alarm in the partition.

Zone

The zone is presented by means of icons indicating the zone status. The icon shows the normal state. The other icons are the same as on the control panel data screen in the “Structure” tab.

Hovering the cursor over the icon will display the zone name in the bottom left corner of the window. Click on the icon to display a pop-up menu. The pop-up menu contains:

•zone name,

•icon indicating the zone status,

•command to bypass / unbypass the zone.

Camera

The link to the camera image is presented by the icon. Hovering the cursor over the icon will display, at the bottom left corner of the window, the name of the camera whose image can be displayed. Click on the icon to display the image from the camera.

The image from the camera will be displayed if a video streaming application is installed in the system.

If the icon overlaps with another item, clicking will display a pop-up menu containing, among other items, the camera name and the command that allows you to display the image from the camera.
